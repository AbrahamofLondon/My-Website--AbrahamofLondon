Excerpt

“They thought they knew the story. But he lived what they only heard in whispers.”

From the dusty streets of Akure to the courtrooms of London, Fathering Without Fear tells the redemptive journey of Abraham of London — a man who refused to disappear. Born a miracle. Shaped by loss. Tested by betrayal. Refined by fire.

From miraculous triplet survival in 1977 Lagos to spiritual awakenings, political campaigns, and family collapse — this is not just a memoir. It’s a movement. A mirror. A map for fathers who won’t be erased.

“This isn’t just a memoir. It’s a declaration. A story they thought they knew — and a father they’ll never forget.”
