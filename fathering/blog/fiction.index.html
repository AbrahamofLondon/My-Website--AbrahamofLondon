The Abraham Chronicles

Where History Meets Imagination

Fantasy Fiction Series • In Development

Where Memoir Ends, Imagination Begins

The Abraham Chronicles is a sweeping fantasy series that takes the courage and determination of the historical Abraham of London and weaves it into a tale of supernatural legacy, generational power, and the ongoing battle between fear and faith across centuries.

What if the courage that drove Abraham across unknown oceans was more than just human determination? What if it was a gift—a supernatural inheritance that passes down through bloodlines, manifesting in each generation's greatest moments of need?

The World of the Chronicles

🌊

Ocean Magic

The Atlantic Ocean holds ancient powers that respond to courage and faith. Ships become conduits for supernatural forces, and crossing the waters awakens dormant abilities in those with the bloodline.

⚡

The Gift of Courage

More than just bravery, the supernatural inheritance manifests as heightened intuition, protective instincts, and the ability to inspire others in moments of crisis. But it comes with a cost.

🌀

Shadow Forces

Ancient entities feed on fear and seek to corrupt the gift for their own purposes. Each generation faces increasingly sophisticated attempts to turn their power toward darkness.

🔮

Generational Echoes

Unresolved traumas and choices echo through bloodlines, manifesting as supernatural challenges. Breaking negative patterns requires both courage and wisdom.

📜

Hidden Histories

Family records, ship logs, and colonial documents contain coded messages and supernatural truths. Research becomes a form of mystical archaeology.

🗡️

Spiritual Warfare

The battle between light and darkness plays out across centuries, with each generation called to defend not just their own families, but the spiritual legacy of courage itself.

Key Characters Across Time

👑

Abraham of London

The Originator (1635)

The first to manifest the gift consciously. A colonial pioneer who discovers his courage has supernatural origins during the perilous Atlantic crossing.

⚔️

Samuel Abraham

The Warrior (1750s)

Abraham's great-grandson who inherits the gift during wartime. Must learn to balance supernatural power with human wisdom.

💔

The Divided Brothers

The Fractured (1860s)

Two brothers whose opposing choices during the Civil War split the family gift and create a supernatural schism that echoes through generations.

🌆

Michael Abraham

The Reluctant (1990s)

A modern descendant who's rejected his family legacy until his children begin manifesting abilities. Must overcome his own fears to guide them.

✨

The Next Generation

The Inheritors (Present)

The current bearers of the gift who must unite centuries of fractured power and finally break the cycle of fear that has haunted the bloodline.

🌑

The Shadow Keeper

The Antagonist (Timeless)

An ancient entity that feeds on inherited fear and trauma, growing stronger with each generation that fails to break free from destructive patterns.
