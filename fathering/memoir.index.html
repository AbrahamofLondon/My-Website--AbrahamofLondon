The Story Behind the Story

What began as research into Abraham of London's colonial journey became a deeply personal exploration of family patterns, generational trauma, and the courage required to break cycles that have persisted for centuries.

This memoir chronicles the journey from a childhood marked by silence and fear to becoming the father I never had—authentic, present, and unafraid of truth. It's about discovering that the same courage that drove Abraham across unknown oceans lives within us today.
